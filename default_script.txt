Welcome to Sarah's fabulous workout.

First exercise: Cat Cow. Get into position on your hands and knees.
Inhale as you arch your back, lifting your head and tailbone. Exhale as you round your spine.

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Great work!
Next exercise: Child's Pose. Sit back on your heels and stretch your arms forward while lowering your chest.
Hold this position and take a deep breath.

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 5 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Next exercise: Balancing Tabletop. Extend your right arm and left leg straight out, then switch sides.
1... Switch... 2... Switch... 3... Switch.

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Next exercise: Spider-Man Plank. Get into a plank position and bring one knee toward the same elbow, alternating sides.
Hold steady and keep your core engaged.

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Next exercise: Shoulder Taps. In a plank position, touch each shoulder with the opposite hand, alternating sides.
Keep your hips steady.

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Next exercise: Up and Down Plank. Start in a forearm plank, press up to a high plank, then return to your forearms.
Keep alternating.

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Next exercise: Dumbbell Crunch. Lie on your back, hold a dumbbell over your chest, and crunch up while engaging your core.

[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Next exercise: Russian Twist with Dumbbell. Sit on the floor, hold the dumbbell with both hands, and twist your torso side to side.

5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
5... 4... 3... 2... 1...

Start in 3... 2... 1...
[Gap: 25 seconds]
